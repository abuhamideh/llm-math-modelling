A factory produces two types of chairs: standard and deluxe.  
Each standard chair requires 3 hours of carpentry and 2 hours of painting.  
Each deluxe chair requires 4 hours of carpentry and 5 hours of painting.  

The factory has 60 hours of carpentry time and 40 hours of painting time available per week.  
The profit is $50 for each standard chair and $80 for each deluxe chair.  

Determine how many chairs of each type the factory should produce to maximize profit.
